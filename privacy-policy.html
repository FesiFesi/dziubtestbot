Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how FEED & FLAP ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Telegram-based game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Telegram User Data

When you interact with our bot, we collect:

  • Telegram user ID (numerical identifier)
  • Username (if public)
  • First name and last name (if provided)
  • Language preference
  • Profile photo (if public)

1.2 Game Data

We collect and store:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • Virtual items and currency balances
  • Game actions and interactions
  • Transaction history within the game
  • Referral and invitation data
  • Time spent playing and session data

1.3 Device and Technical Information

  • IP address and geolocation (approximate)
  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser information
  • Access times and dates
  • Error logs and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Game Functionality: Providing core game features, saving progress, managing virtual economies
  • User Experience: Personalizing content, improving gameplay, technical support
  • Analytics: Understanding user behavior, improving game balance, identifying popular features
  • Security: Preventing fraud, detecting unauthorized access, enforcing terms of service
  • Communication: Sending game-related notifications, updates, and promotional materials
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements, responding to legal requests

3. Telegram Integration

Our game operates through the Telegram Bot API. We:

  • Access only the information you choose to share with our bot
  • Do not read your private messages with other users
  • Cannot access your contacts or other Telegram data
  • May send you messages related to gameplay
  • Use Telegram's secure messaging infrastructure

4. TON Wallet and Cryptocurrency

For cryptocurrency features:

  • We may collect wallet addresses for reward distribution
  • Transaction data is recorded on the TON blockchain (public ledger)
  • We do not store private keys or seed phrases
  • Cryptocurrency transactions are irreversible
  • We may track reward distribution for tax and legal compliance

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, payment processing, customer support
  • Analytics Services: Google Analytics, Telegram Analytics, custom analytics platforms
  • Advertising Partners: For targeted advertising and performance measurement
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

We do not sell personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes.

6. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures

Data Retention: We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Game data may be retained longer for analytics and legal compliance.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing
  • Objection: Object to data processing for certain purposes
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: feed.and.flap@gmail.com

8. Children's Privacy

Our game is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected information from someone under 18, we will delete that information immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Other legally approved transfer mechanisms

10. Advertising and Marketing

We may display personalized advertisements based on your interests and game activity. You may:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request information about advertising data collection
  • Block our bot to stop receiving promotional messages

11. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Session management and user authentication
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Personalization and user preferences
  • Security and fraud prevention

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through:

  • In-game notifications
  • Messages through our Telegram bot
  • Updates on our website

Your continued use of the game after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Data Breach Notification

In case of a data breach affecting personal information, we will:

  • Notify relevant supervisory authorities within 72 hours (where required)
  • Inform affected users if the breach poses high risk
  • Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach
